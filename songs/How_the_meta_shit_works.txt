"version": Used to indacate what versions the songs are from and what versions are ported.
"classic": Used to turn off the v3 healthbar and have the kade hud.
"port": What freeplay portrait the freeplay gets for that song.
"modlink": What link opens when you press shift on the song in freeplay.
"normalport": Tweens the port like it's an offical one in the fan-made section.
"noHealthBar": Turns off just the v3 healthbar.
